Under the supervision of the County Commissioners and County Clerk, this office offers information, referral and provision of emergency aid to low-income/indigent clientele. This office provides assistance for basic, reasonable, non-medical necessities and necessary medical services to qualified applicants. The county is the resource of last resort for Latah County residents only.
