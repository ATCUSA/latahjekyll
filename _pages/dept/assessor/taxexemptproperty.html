The Latah County Board of Commissioners adopted Ordinance No. 265, which sets up a standardized procedure to approve property tax exemptions annually.

Application deadline is April 15th.

The Exemption Renewal forms, applications and worksheets are located in the Forms and Files section.

Why does Latah County require exempt property owners to apply again to continue receiving a property tax exemption?

Idaho Code 63-602 states that all exemptions shall be approved annually by the county board of equalization.

How does Latah County's ordinance implement this requirement?

Beginning in 2006, exempt property owners must complete an application that documents ownership, nature of the use of the property, and, if applicable, purpose and source of revenue for the owning organization. This standardized process will also be applied to all new requests for exemption this year and in the future. Beginning in 2007, owners of property that is already exempt will re-apply annually by completing a simple one-page form certifying that the ownership and use of the property remains unchanged.

Who approves property tax exemptions?

The Latah County Board of Commissioners grant or deny all property tax exemptions.

When does the Board of County Commissioners consider exemptions?

The Commissioners consider exemption applications through April 15th.
