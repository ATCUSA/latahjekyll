For details of which specific districts, cities, and any possible elections please see the 2017 Election Calendar on the Elections Home Page under Election Links.

Note: *When a deadline happens on a Saturday or Sunday the date is extended until the following business day. Our goal is to get absentee ballots mailed to you as soon as possible so that they can be returned in time to be counted.

Please be sure to complete the FPCA including all the necessary information and identification required by the State of Idaho, Email addresses and complete telephone numbers are very helpful in the absentee voting process.

REMEMBER: Voter registration closes 25 days before every election date.
