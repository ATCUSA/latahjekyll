Moscow Recycling

There is a minimum $10.00 fee for allloads of disposal. Up to 200 lbs household waste, 460 lbs demolition, or 280 lbs mixed.

Inert/Demolition Waste - Includes large yard waste, furniture, mattresses, carpeting, and untreated construction debris, etc.

Household Waste - Includes plastic/rubber materials, paper, food, styrofoam, treated lumber, animal hides and waste, medical waste, fluorescent bulbs, etc.
