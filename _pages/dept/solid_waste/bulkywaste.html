The Bulky Waste is a program for FREE disposal of Inert/Demolition Waste. This includes furniture, mattresses, metal, tree stumps, branches, carpeting, roofing material and demolition waste (untreated lumber, concrete, ceramic/porcelain, sheetrock) and up to 4 passenger tires and 2 appliances. Sites do not accept: E-waste, household waste, small appliances or any plastic items. All residential accounts should use weekly driveway service for household garbage. Users must log in with the applicable property address.
