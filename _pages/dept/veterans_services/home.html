This office provides assistance to veterans and dependents, under the direction of the County Commissioners and the County Clerk. They assist, at no fee to the veteran, in the preparation of forms and supporting evidence to be submitted to the US Department of Veterans Affairs or the Idaho State Division of Veterans Service when applying for veteransâ€™ benefits which include disability compensation, pensions, and medical and death benefits. Information is also provided on how to access the Spokane VA Medical Center and the Mobile Health Clinic. Call to make an appointment.

Office hours are 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Closed Noon to 1:00 p.m. Monday through Friday.
