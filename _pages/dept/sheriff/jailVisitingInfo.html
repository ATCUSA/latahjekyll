Visiting Hours for Male Inmates

1pm to 3pm on Tuesday
7pm to 9pm on Thursday
1pm to 4pm on Sunday

Visiting Hours for Female Inmates

7pm to 9pm on Monday
1pm to 3pm on Thursday
1pm to 3pm on Saturday

Be sure to bring a valid picture ID when coming to the Jail. Any juveniles coming to visit an inmate must be accompanied by their parent or legal guardian and proof of such. Visitors must not have been incarcerated within the last 30 days. If you are on probation you must have prior approval through your probation officer.
