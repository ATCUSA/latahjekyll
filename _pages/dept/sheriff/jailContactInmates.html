Inmates have access to telephones to make outgoing collect calls only. You may set up an account by contacting Telmate at (ph.) 866-516-0115 or online at www.gettingout.com If you call the Jail, you will not be able to reach an inmate. To put money on an inmate account you may come into the Sheriff’s Office and use the Kiosk in the lobby, the phone number and website listed above or mail in a money order made out to the inmate. Personal checks are not allowed. The Sheriff’s Office will not accept property, mail or medications brought in without prior authorization by the Jail Commander.
