Sexual abuse and sexual harassment seriously hampers the mission of the Latah County Jail to provide a safe and secure environment for the inmates and staff.  To comply with training standards in accordance with the Federal PREA (Prison Rape Elimination Act) rule. The Latah County Jail has zero tolerance with regard to sexual abuse and sexual harassment in this facility and will take appropriate affirmative measures to protect all inmates from sexual abuse and harassment and to promptly and thoroughly investigate all allegations of sexual abuse and sexual harassment.

The Latah County Jail has developed policy and procedures to reduce the risk of in-custody sexual assaults and will ensure that an investigation is completed for all allegations of sexual abuse and sexual harassment.

If you were a victim or if you suspect abuse has happened in this facility, you are urged to call the Latah County Jail at 208-883-5705 and ask to speak to a supervisor. Reports may also be made in writing, anonymously and from third parties. Report anonymously, if you want, to an outside agency by calling the Nez Perce County Adult Detention Facility at 208-799-3139.
