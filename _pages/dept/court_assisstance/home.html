This office provides the public self-help access to legal information, social services, and assistance with standard court forms for routine civil cases in state court. This office does not provide services for post-conviction criminal matters or cases against Latah County.

This office deals with cases in State courts. To access information about Idaho's Federal Courts, including frequently asked questions about bankruptcy, please see www.id.uscourts.gov.
