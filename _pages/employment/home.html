Regional QuickFacts

According to 2010 U.S. Census Bureau information, Latah County has a population of 37,244. Find more Latah County statistics.

The City of Moscow has a population of approximately 23,800 and is the location of the University of Idaho.

The City of Pullman is located 7 miles west of Moscow in the state of Washington, has a population of approximately 29,799 and is the location of Washington State University

Current Employment Opportunities

Latah County is an Equal Opportunity Employer

Employment selection and related decisions are made without regard to race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected class.

Veterans’ Preference

Latah County provides veterans’ preference in appointment to preference eligible applicants as defined by Idaho Code, Title 65, Chapter 5. If you wish to seek this preference, please complete the information on the employment application and provide a copy of your DD214 and, if applicable, letter from the Department of Veterans Affairs stating your disability determination. Documentation must be received before the closing date of the position you are applying for.
