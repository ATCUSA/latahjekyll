Planning

The Latah County Planning Commission consists of 7 members who are appointed by the Latah County Board of Commissioners. These individuals review and recommend changes to the Latah County Comprehensive Plan and the Latah County Land Use Ordinance.

To submit general comments to the Planning Commission, please e-mail Michelle Fuson. To submit comments regarding a public hearing before the Zoning Commission, please refer to the public hearing packet below on how to submit comments. To receive e-mails with links to new agendas for the Planning and Zoning Commissions please subscribe here.
